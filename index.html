The Top 5 Places to Visit in the Philippines


The Philippines is a beautiful place to visit due to its beaches, historical landmarks, and its vast amount of nature. The people are hospitable and kind, even to foreigners; and speak English, which is a universal language most people can speak in. If you wish to travel abroad to the Philippines, here are the top 5 wonders that are a must-visit to the lovely foreigners.



Number 5: Bohol

Bohol is a province located in the state of Visayas. It is very popular amongst travelers for its beaches and greenery. You can find aetas on the beach you can take a picture with; Tarsiers, a nocturnal endangered species, in the wild; and lastly, the popular chocolate hills which is brown during the dry season and green during the wet seasons. There are a lot of activities you can do on the shores of Bohol and it will make your trip very eventful and memorable.


Number 4: Palawan

Palawan is an island province located in the state of Luzon. It is well-known for its crystal clear beaches, marine wildlife, and island-hopping. The beaches are the favorite of many foreigners, especially South Koreans. You can do many things in Palawan, such as scuba diving for wildlife and reefs , boating, and much more. With the huge list of activities that can be accomplished, it will make your trip very eventful and memorable.


Number 3: Baguio

If you're not a fan of a tropical vacation, go to Baguio, a city found in Northern Luzon. Although the road from Manila to Baguio is long, the trip is worth it as you are able to see many famous landmarks such as the Lion's Head, a statue located along Kennon Road, which is a road part of the route to Baguio; and many cliffs and greenery. Being on top of a mountain, Baguio is cold, which is perfect for tourists who come from colder climates. Even if there are no beaches, there are many activities one can do in Baguio such as strawberry-picking from Baguio's strawberry farms, visit the monuments and learn more about Filipino history, or ride the swan boats and take a stroll in nature at Burnham Park. Just like the previous items, there are many things one can do in Baguio, making a memorable and enjoyable trip.

Number 2: Davao

If you want to explore other lands in the Philippines aside from Luzon and Visayas, try Davao City. Davao is a city located in Mindanao. It has many monuments and interesting places and parks, such as Eden Park, where one can bike across a cliff, or learn more about the history of the Philippines by visiting its many monuments or dropping by the Davao Museum. Another good thing about Davao is that you can hunt for pearls in its special "Pearl Farm", whose title is self-explanatory. For tourists who are fellow muslims and would like to travel to Mindanao, Davao is the way to go.


Number 1: Cebu

Aside from Palawan, you can find one of the cleanest beaches in Cebu. Cebu is a city located in Visayas, and it has much more to it aside from beaches. There are shrines, monuments, and many luxurious hotels for a foreigner to rest in. Just like the others, you can learn more about the detailed backstory of our country and be amazed by the tiny details found on our monuments, and at the same time buy souvenirs on the beachside before you take a dive. For foreigners who want convinience stores nearby for just a trip to the beach, Cebu is your choice.
